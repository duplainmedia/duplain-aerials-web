06 · Common questions

What other boards have asked.

Will you do the full compliance interpretation? +

Standard scope is imaging and the permit overlay. The interpretation of compliance status remains a conversation between your board, your pond manager, and the county. GIS overlay analysis is an optional add-on. We do not represent your community to the county; that role stays with your management firm.

Do we own the deliverables? +

Yes. PDF and imagery archive go to your management firm directly. We keep a copy only to support next year's flight. If you change vendors, your archive walks with you.

What do residents need to know? +

A short notice two to three days before the flight, sent through whatever channel your board normally uses. We can supply a draft. No homes are photographed at recognizable resolution; incidental frames are cropped or blurred before delivery.

When is the best time to capture? +

Late summer through fall, after the wet season has loaded the system. Day-of, 8 to 10 AM is the sweet spot for wind, glare, and thermal turbulence. We reschedule when weather does not cooperate.

Are you Part 107 and insured? +

Yes. FAA Part 107 current. $1M commercial liability through Skywatch. Certificate of Insurance issued to your management firm before any flight, listing your HOA and the management firm as additional insured.

What about pricing year over year? +

Per pond count, all-in. The first year carries the discovery and overlay-build cost; subsequent years use the same baseline maps and price lower. Schedule below; written quote on request before any commitment.
